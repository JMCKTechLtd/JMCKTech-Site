How BlockShell Lite works

Same visual builder across editions – drag blocks, preview the script, then use it in your own labs and environments.

1. Drag & connect blocks
Choose from pre-built blocks for common tasks and snap them together to design the workflow you want.
2. Preview the script
See the generated script update live as you add, remove or reorder blocks in the workspace.
3. Use it in your labs
Export the script and run it in your own lab environment, training course, or practice setup.
