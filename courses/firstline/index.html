Firstline Labs

  • Lab 1: Ticket Viewing & Navigation
    Task: Practice navigating the different modules, switch between List and Dashboard , and view/update record details. Your changes persist while the page is open.
  • Lab 2: Ticket Editing & Status Updates
    Task: Edit incident tickets and update their status. Primary functions (selection & editing) are untouched. You can also switch modules and view a lightweight dashboard.
  • Lab 3: Work Notes & Comments
    Task: Practice adding work notes to tickets. Click on a ticket to view its details and activity timeline. Add work notes to document your progress and communicate with team members. Watch how new notes appear in the timeline.
  • Lab 4: Assignment & Escalation
    Task: Practice assigning tickets and escalating critical issues. Select tickets using checkboxes, then use the Assign button to reassign them. For high-priority unresolved tickets, practice escalation procedures to ensure timely resolution.
  • Lab 5: Ticket Resolution & Closure
    Task: Practice resolving and closing tickets. Select in-progress tickets and use the Resolve button to mark them as resolved with a solution. Then practice closing resolved tickets after confirming user satisfaction. Watch how tickets move through the resolution lifecycle.
