Lab 1 - Send As

Outlook — Send As (Shared Mailbox)

Learn how to send emails from a shared mailbox using Outlook's "Send As" feature. This is commonly used for support teams, sales teams, and other shared email addresses.

  1. Click the New Email button in the navigation bar to open the compose dialog.
  2. In the compose dialog, click on the From dropdown menu and select Support Team (support@blockshell.app) as the sender.
  3. Fill in the recipient's email address in the To field and add a subject line.
  4. Click the Send button to send the email and watch for the success confirmation message.
