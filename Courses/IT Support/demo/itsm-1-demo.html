Learning Objectives

In this lab, you will learn to:

Lab Requirements

2 Search for Network Issue Pending

3 Open Ticket Details Pending

Click on a ticket to view its full details in the modal window.

4 Review Information Pending

Review the ticket priority, status, assignment, and description.

5 Close Modal Pending

Close the ticket detail modal to return to the list view.
